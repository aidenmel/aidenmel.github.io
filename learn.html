      What are Scroll Effects?

      Scroll effects are animations or manipulations that occur when the client reaches ("scrolls pass") a specific point on the page. Most effects are integrated with javascript, though some can occur with pure css.

      1. Appear/Disappear FX

      A commonly adopted scroll effect that enables and disables elements.

      How does it work?

      This works by comparing the object's absolute position to that of the cilent's active position (the position at which the client has scrolled). So, if the scroll position is greater than the element's absolute position, show the object. Else, hide the object.
